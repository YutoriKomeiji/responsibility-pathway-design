# RPD Worked Case Package Guide v0.1

## Purpose

This guide connects the empirical research protocol to reusable case-study artifacts.

## Package sequence

```text
research question
  → empirical study record
  → worked or selected case
  → comparative scorecard
  → observation logs
  → result report
  → claim and theory revision
```

## Included artifacts

1. [`ai-assisted-benefit-review-worked-case-v0.1.md`](../examples/ai-assisted-benefit-review-worked-case-v0.1.md) — synthetic comparison of three designs for an AI-assisted benefit-review pathway.
2. [`rpd-comparative-case-scorecard-v0.1.md`](../templates/rpd-comparative-case-scorecard-v0.1.md) — structured comparison across pathway properties, burdens, scenarios, and anti-theatre checks.
3. [`rpd-observation-log-v0.1.md`](../templates/rpd-observation-log-v0.1.md) — time-ordered separation of observable events from interpretation.
4. [`rpd-study-result-report-v0.1.md`](../templates/rpd-study-result-report-v0.1.md) — bounded reporting of findings, counterevidence, rival explanations, limitations, and theory consequences.

These complement the [`RPD Empirical Study Record`](../templates/rpd-empirical-study-record-v0.1.md), which should normally be completed before data collection.

## Minimum use rules

- preserve a credible comparator;
- declare pathway and affected-party scope;
- separate design, implementation, exercise, operation, and broader validation;
- record negative and null findings;
- preserve disagreement and counterevidence;
- disclose burdens and who bears them;
- distinguish observation from interpretation;
- define what would narrow, suspend, or retire a claim;
- stop at the relevant human gate before field use, publication, or operational authorization.

## Evidence discipline

A worked case may demonstrate internal method coherence. It does not provide empirical validation merely because every template is complete.

A score is not evidence by itself. Every score must trace to an observation, source, exercise result, or declared judgment with limitations.

A successful exercise does not establish live operational performance. A live operational result does not automatically establish broader legal, social, ethical, or affected-party acceptance.

## Recommended review order

1. Check the initial pathway and diagnosis.
2. Check whether each alternative is credible.
3. Check requirement traceability.
4. Examine actor absence, time pressure, evidence failure, and cross-organizational disagreement.
5. Review burden distribution and access.
6. Examine negative and null results.
7. Identify rival explanations.
8. Apply retain, refine, restrict, revise, suspend, or retire decisions.

## Research boundary

This package supports research preparation and structured comparison. It is not a certification scheme, legal analysis, deployment authorization, or proof of safety, fairness, compliance, effectiveness, superiority, production readiness, or social acceptance.
