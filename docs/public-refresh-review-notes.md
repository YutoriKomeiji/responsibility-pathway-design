# Public Surface Refresh Review Notes

This change set modernizes the public entry surface of Responsibility Pathway Design while preserving its current research and authority boundaries.

## Review goals

- make the RPM → RPD → RPE relationship visible on first contact;
- distinguish diagnosis, design translation, and implementation-adjacent engineering;
- improve search-result comprehension through a precise opening description;
- provide goal-oriented reading paths;
- expose research status and non-claims near the top level;
- distinguish technical rollback from responsibility recovery;
- present contestability, affected-party accessibility, residual stewardship, proportionality, and anti-theatre controls as first-class design dimensions.

## What this refresh does not do

- approve RPD as a completed academic theory;
- claim empirical validation, legal validity, certification, safety, fairness, compliance, or production readiness;
- approve a paper submission or standards proposal;
- replace the existing detailed documents;
- change canonical human authority.

3. create a pattern and anti-pattern index;

5. introduce Japanese-language navigation without allowing terminology drift;

7. review repository metadata, social preview, issue templates, and contribution guidance.
