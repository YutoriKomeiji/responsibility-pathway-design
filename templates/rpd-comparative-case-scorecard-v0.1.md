## Scale

- `0` — absent or unsupported;
- `1` — weak, nominal, or mostly documentary;
- `2` — partial and condition-dependent;
- `3` — strong within the declared design or observed scope;
- `N/O` — not observed;
- `N/A` — not applicable with justification.

Every score requires evidence, limitation, and reviewer-confidence fields.

## Interpretation discipline

Record separately:

- observed difference;
- plausible mechanism;
- rival explanation;
- scope of inference;
- missing evidence;
- whether the result concerns design, implementation, exercise, operation, or broader validation.

## Prohibited use

Do not use this scorecard as:

- a certification label;
- a legal or compliance determination;
- a global system score;
- proof of safety, fairness, effectiveness, or production readiness;
- a substitute for affected-party participation or domain-specific review.
